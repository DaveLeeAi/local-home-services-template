# Permits & Building Codes in Atlanta

## Do I Need a Permit?
Yes, most deck projects in the Atlanta Metro area require a building permit. This ensures the structure is safe and meets code.

### When is a permit required?
- If the deck is more than 30 inches off the ground.
- If the deck is attached to the house.
- If the total footprint exceeds a certain square footage (varies by county).
- For any structural repairs.
