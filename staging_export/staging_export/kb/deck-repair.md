# Deck Repair Services

## Signs You Need a Repair
- **Rotting Wood**: Soft spots, discoloration, or fungus growth, especially on posts and joists.
- **Loose Railings**: Shaky or unstable railings are a major safety hazard.
- **Cracked Boards**: Splits and cracks can cause splinters and weaken the structure.
- **Rusted Hardware**: Corroded nails, screws, and fasteners can lead to structural failure.

## Our Repair Process
1. **Safety Inspection**: We check the structural integrity of the ledger board, posts, and framing.
2. **Board Replacement**: We match new boards to your existing deck as closely as possible.
3. **Structural Reinforcement**: Adding blocking or sistering joists where needed.
4. **Stair Repair**: Fixing wobbly stringers and treads.

## Cost
Repairs typically range from $500 for minor fixes to $3,000+ for structural work.
